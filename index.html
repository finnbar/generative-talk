Generative Music

Or: why it is both easy and hard for computers to generate music.

Generative Music?

  • Brian Eno made it happen!

I'll put a quote here.

  • There are three different avenues we're going to consider:
    • Rule-based combination of musical phrases
    • Generation of new musical phrases using music theory
    • Beyond music theory

Thesis Statement:

It's easy to generate music that sounds fine and pleasant, but very hard to generate music that actually provokes emotion.

Rule-based Combination

  • Mozart's Dice Game (1793)
  • Simply roll a pair of dice and pick the corresponding bar of music.
  • Different set of bars to choose from based on how many times you've rolled.
  • All sounds pretty similar though...

Source: Mozart's original publication (modified), hosted on playonlinedicegames.com
