Rule-based Combination

We have some phrases of music and use rules to combine them.

  • Mozart's Dice Game (1793)
  • Roll dice to pick phrases.
  • Different phrases to choose from based on how many times you've rolled.
  • Problem: output pieces sound very similar.

Source: Mozart's original publication (modified), hosted on playonlinedicegames.com

Melodies from Scratch

Let's pick notes and build melodies out of them.

  • Here's a scale. Hooray!
  • We can then assign numbers to these notes.
  • New task: make phrases as streams of numbers.
  • It sounds... okay

Notably we've only considered pitch, not rhythm.

Solution: we introduce a grid, and go through each block of notes in turn.
